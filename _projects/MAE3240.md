This questions asks to find the temperature at the surface, a value for heat generation, the thermal conductivity and to plot a temperature profile. We assume that the wall has 1 dimensional heat transfer and is at steady state. 

I learned from this homework that it is important to state assumptions at the beginning, to convey what simplications we will be making since that affects the complexity of how the problem is solved and results in clearer communication. I also learned how to use these assumptions to simplify equations and how to use boundary conditions to solve for unknown variables.
